📖 How to Play

  • 🕹️Move with Arrow Keys or WASD — on mobile, drag anywhere on the screen.
  • 🚫The Forbidden Color is shown in the top bar — never touch those blocks!
  • ✅All other colored blocks are safe to pass through freely.
  • ⏱Watch the countdown bar — it drains fast! A brief flash previews the next color. React quickly!
  • 🔥Surviving each color change builds your combo for big bonus points.
  • ⚡Collect glowing spinning stars for power-ups: Shield, Slow Time, Clear blocks, or Score ×2.
  • 💫Dodge very close to a dangerous block for a Near Miss bonus!
  • ♿Colorblind mode adds hatch patterns & symbols to dangerous blocks. High Contrast boosts visual separation.
