How to Play

🕹️

Move Fast

Use arrow keys or WASD on desktop. On mobile, drag anywhere on screen to move your player.

🚫

Avoid Danger

The forbidden color is highlighted as a glowing swatch at the top of the screen. Never touch blocks of that color. All other colors are safe to pass through.

🪙

Collect Coins

Pick up glowing coins as you dodge. One pickup = one coin. Spend them in the Shop on skins and power-up upgrades.

⚡

Panic Mode

The game gets faster and harder as time goes on. The forbidden color shifts periodically — react quickly to every change.
