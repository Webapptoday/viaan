How to Play

  • Move with Arrow Keys or WASD — on mobile, drag anywhere on the screen.
  • The Forbidden Color is shown in the top bar — never touch those blocks.
  • All other colored blocks are safe to pass through freely.
  • Watch the countdown bar — it drains fast. A brief flash previews the next color. React quickly.
  • Surviving each color change builds your combo for bonus points.
  • Collect glowing power-ups: Shield, Slow Time, Clear Screen, or Score ×2.
  • Dodge very close to a dangerous block for a Near Miss bonus.
  • Colorblind mode adds patterns & symbols to dangerous blocks. High Contrast boosts visual separation.
